Hi! I'm going to tell you about my Favorite hobby, basketball!

Basketball is my favorite sport because it is fast paced and there are always plays going on. Basketball can be played indoor and outdoor so you can play it year round. Attributes in the sport include speed, agility, strength, and vertical{how high you can jump}.
